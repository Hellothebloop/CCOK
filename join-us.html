Join Our Team

The Counseling Center of Keyport is growing, and we're looking for dedicated mental health professionals to join our collaborative practice.

Led by Molly Hahn, an experienced and skilled mentor, our center provides a supportive environment where clinicians can thrive professionally while delivering exceptional care to our community.

Whether you're an established professional or a recent graduate beginning your career, we offer the resources, flexibility, and collegial atmosphere you need to do your best work. Our beautifully renovated office space in central Keyport provides a welcoming setting for individual, couples, and group therapy, with easy access for both clinicians and clients.
